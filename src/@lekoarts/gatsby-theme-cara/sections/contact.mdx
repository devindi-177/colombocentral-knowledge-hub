> AIESEC in Colombo Central. 

AIESEC in University of Colombo, also known as AIESEC in Colombo Central, is a part of AIESEC in Sri Lanka that strives for youth leadership development by involving them in cross-cultural activities so that they could make a positive impact on their communities as well as the world.
